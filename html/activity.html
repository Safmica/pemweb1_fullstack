EXTRACURRICULAR

SCIENCE CLUB

Explore the wonders of science and unlock your curiosity with the Ersanawata Science Club! Dive into exciting experiments, innovative projects, and thought-provoking discussions that bring classroom theories to life. Whether you're passionate about physics, biology, chemistry, or technology, the Science Club offers a platform to innovate, collaborate, and compete in national and international events. Guided by expert mentors, you'll gain hands-on experience and develop problem-solving skills that prepare you for a future of endless possibilities. Fuel your passion for discovery—join the Science Club and make an impact!

MUSIC CLUB

Unleash your musical talent and join the vibrant Ersanawata Music Club! Whether you're a singer, instrumentalist, or just someone who loves music, this is the place to express yourself and collaborate with like-minded peers. Learn, create, and perform music across various genres while honing your skills under the guidance of experienced mentors. From live performances to exciting workshops, every moment in the Music Club is a step toward discovering your potential and sharing the joy of music with the school community. Let the rhythm move you—join us today!

THEATER

Step into the spotlight and let your creativity shine with the Ersanawata Theater Club! Whether you're an aspiring actor, playwright, director, or stage crew member, this club is your stage to express emotions and tell compelling stories. Engage in acting workshops, scriptwriting sessions, and thrilling stage performances that captivate audiences. Build confidence, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for the performing arts. Your story deserves to be told—join the Theater Club and bring it to life!

SPORTS CLUB

Embrace the spirit of teamwork and athleticism with the Ersanawata Sports Club! From basketball and soccer to volleyball and athletics, there's a place for every sports enthusiast to compete, learn, and grow. Train with skilled coaches, participate in inter-school tournaments, and achieve personal and team victories. Beyond the games, you'll develop discipline, resilience, and lifelong friendships. Get active, stay fit, and represent your school with pride—join the Sports Club today!

DEBATE CLUB

Speak up, think critically, and make your voice heard in the Ersanawata Debate Club! This club is perfect for students passionate about expressing their opinions, analyzing issues, and mastering public speaking. Join weekly debates, attend workshops, and compete in regional and national debate tournaments. Gain confidence, improve your communication skills, and learn the art of persuasive argumentation. Empower yourself to lead conversations and shape ideas.

CHEERLEADER

Spread school spirit and shine with energy in the Ersanawata Cheerleader Club! This club combines athleticism, rhythm, and teamwork as you master dynamic cheer routines, stunts, and chants. Perform at school events, sports games, and competitions while building confidence, coordination, and friendships. With a supportive team and high-energy atmosphere, you’ll make every event unforgettable.

LANGUAGE CLUB

Discover the beauty of languages and cultures with the Ersanawata Language Club! Whether you're interested in learning new languages, improving fluency, or exploring global traditions, this club offers interactive sessions, fun activities, and cultural exchanges. Participate in language workshops, conversation clubs, and competitions that sharpen your skills and broaden your horizons. Celebrate the diversity of communication—join the Language Club and connect with the world!

MATH CLUB

Unlock the beauty of numbers and explore the limitless possibilities of mathematics with the Ersanawata Math Club! This club is a haven for curious minds who enjoy solving puzzles, tackling challenges, and discovering real-world applications of math. Participate in math olympiads, fun competitions, and hands-on workshops that sharpen your analytical and problem-solving skills. Whether you're a math enthusiast or just want to improve your skills, join the Math Club and make math exciting and rewarding!
