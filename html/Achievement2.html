About ERSANAWATA

ERSANAWATA High School is a well-known educational institution dedicated to shaping outstanding young individuals. Founded with a mission to provide quality education, ERSANAWATA fosters a learning environment that emphasizes both academic excellence and personal growth.


ERSANAWATA High School encourages students to pursue their passions and excel in diverse areas of study, helping them build a foundation of integrity, responsibility, creativity, and perseverance. With a strong commitment to character-building, ERSANAWATA guides students to become responsible, knowledgeable, and compassionate members of society.
