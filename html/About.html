SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

ERSANAWATA

ERSANAWATA Senior High School is an international-level institution dedicated to preparing students for a global future. Offering two main programs, Natural Sciences (IPA) and Social Sciences (IPS), the school combines a modern curriculum with expert educators and excellent facilities to provide a well-rounded educational experience.

With a focus on academic excellence and character development, ERSANAWATA empowers students to reach their full potential. By fostering creativity, integrity, and innovation, the school equips future leaders to make meaningful contributions to society on a global scale.

HISTORY OF

ERSANAWATA HIGH SCHOOL

Established in 1985, ERSANAWATA High School began as a small educational institution with a vision to provide quality education and nurture future leaders. Founded by a group of dedicated educators and community leaders, the school initially operated in a modest building with just a handful of students and teachers.


Over the years, ERSANAWATA grew steadily, becoming a beacon of academic excellence and character development. By the early 2000s, the school expanded its facilities, incorporating state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, and a library that housed thousands of books and digital resources.


The name "ERSANAWATA" symbolizes unity, wisdom, and perseveranceâ€”values that have been deeply ingrained in the school's culture. The school has always prioritized holistic education, blending academic achievements with extracurricular activities such as arts, sports, and community service.


Today, ERSANAWATA High School is a proud institution with a diverse community of students and alumni who excel in various fields. Its reputation as a center for excellence continues to inspire generations to strive for knowledge, integrity, and innovation.

Welcome Message from the Principal of ERSANAWATA Senior High School On behalf of the entire ERSANAWATA Senior High School community, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our school. Our vision is to foster the development of students who are not only academically excellent but also ethically grounded and prepared to become the future leaders of our society. At ERSANAWATA, we pride ourselves on creating a nurturing environment where every student can thrive, excel, and pursue their dreams.


Our school is committed to offering a balanced education that integrates both academic knowledge and personal development. Through our competency-based curriculum, we ensure that students acquire the necessary skills and values that will serve them well in the 21st century. We also place a strong emphasis on fostering creativity, critical thinking, and social responsibility.


At ERSANAWATA, we believe in the importance of a holistic approach to education. This is why we offer state-of-the-art facilities, extracurricular activities, and leadership development programs designed to help students grow beyond the classroom. We are dedicated to cultivating not only outstanding scholars but also compassionate individuals who will contribute positively to the world.


As a school, we are also committed to creating a supportive environment where teachers are not just instructors but mentors who guide students through their academic and personal journeys. Together, with the support of our dedicated educators, staff, and parents, we strive to provide an exceptional learning experience that prepares students for both challenges and opportunities in life.

We invite you to explore everything that ERSANAWATA Senior High School has to offer, and we look forward to working together to help our students achieve their full potential.
