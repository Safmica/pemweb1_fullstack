HISTORY OF

ERSANAWATA HIGH SCHOOL

Established in 1985, ERSANAWATA High School began as a small educational institution with a vision to provide quality education and nurture future leaders. Founded by a group of dedicated educators and community leaders, the school initially operated in a modest building with just a handful of students and teachers.


Over the years, ERSANAWATA grew steadily, becoming a beacon of academic excellence and character development. By the early 2000s, the school expanded its facilities, incorporating state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, and a library that housed thousands of books and digital resources.

Today, ERSANAWATA High School is a proud institution with a diverse community of students and alumni who excel in various fields. Its reputation as a center for excellence continues to inspire generations to strive for knowledge, integrity, and innovation.
